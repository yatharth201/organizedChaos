ORGANISED CHAOS

Organized Chaos presents a visualization of the star-centric system in Indian popular cinema. The great majority of Bollywood stars are connected via family networks, formed over several decades. The lack of regulations in the film industry made it a lucrative opportunity for a fast return of capital during WWII. The studios, with their extensive infrastructure, could not compete with the flow of independent capital and slowly disappeared, making way for the ad hoc style of filmmaking. Actors and actresses became the biggest beneficiaries of the freelancing system, and with time, this star-centric system became a dynastic system. This unique feature of Bollywood and Indian popular cinema is the subject of Organized Chaos.
